Our Heritage

Founded in 2022 by a collective of passionate master roasters, The Brew emerged from a singular vision: to strip away the noise of traditional cafes and reorient the focus entirely onto deep, dark, rich extractions.

We began as an underground pop-up experiment operating during late-night hours. Today, our brick-and-mortar storefront services hundreds of creators daily, keeping a tight standard on roasting cycles and extraction ratios.

Sourcing Standards

We strictly trade directly with high-altitude farming collectives across East Africa and Central America. By bypassing generic bulk auction channels, we pay above fair-trade thresholds to source specialty micro-lots evaluated at 85+ SCA sensory scores.
