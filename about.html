Our Heritage & Corporate Agenda

Founded in 2022 by three tech dropouts who realized charging $6 for water and beans was vastly more profitable than building actual software.

The Brew reorients premium coffee back onto rich, deep extraction frameworks. We treat roasting like a mad science experiment and extraction like a hostage negotiation. Our mission is to extract every bit of value out of the bean—and out of your credit card.

Our baristas are certified espresso psychologists, trained to judge your fashion choices within two seconds of you walking through our minimalist steel door frames. We do not apologize for our high prices; we apologize for the fact that you still aren't energetic enough to secure that promotion.
