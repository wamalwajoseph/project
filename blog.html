How to Dial-In an Espresso at Home (And Pretend You're Happy)

Published June 14, 2026 by Marcus Vance

Dialing in your espresso is all about balancing variables: dry weight, liquid output, and extraction speed. We recommend a clean 1:2 ratio to maximize richness. If your shot pulls under 20 seconds, it's under-extracted, sour, and completely uninspired—just like your current dating profile. Adjust your grinder, buy real beans, and try not to spill any on your designer rug.
