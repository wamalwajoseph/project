How to Dial-In an Espresso at Home

Published June 14, 2026 by Marcus Vance

Dialing in your espresso is all about balancing variables: dry coffee weight, liquid brew output, and extraction speed. We recommend a clean 1:2 ratio to maximize richness and flavor clarity. If your shot pulls under 20 seconds, it's under-extracted and sour. Carefully adjust your grinder profile finer, utilize fresh beans, and ensure even distribution inside your portafilter basket for consistent results.
