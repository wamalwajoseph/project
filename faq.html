Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shelf life of your whole bean bags?

We recommend enjoying whole beans within 4 to 5 weeks from the stamped roast date for peak aromatics. Storing them in an airtight canister away from direct sunlight will keep flavor profiles consistent.

Do you support direct trade with international farms?

Yes, all of our roasted coffee lots are sourced directly from sustainable farms, ensuring equitable compensation for our agricultural partners globally.
